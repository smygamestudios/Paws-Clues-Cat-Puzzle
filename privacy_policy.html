This Privacy Policy describes how SmyGameStudios collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile puzzle game (the "Game"). By playing the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01 Information We Collect

Anonymous Account

When you launch the Game, we automatically create an anonymous account via Firebase Authentication. No personal information is required to play.

Optional Google Account Linking

If you choose to link your anonymous account to your Google account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (first and last name)
  • Profile photo URL

This is entirely optional and can be done or undone at any time in the Game's settings.

Game Progress Data

To power leaderboards and sync your progress, we store:

  • Experience points (XP) and current level number
  • In-game coin balance
  • Music and sound effect preferences (on/off)
  • Other gameplay state and settings

Device & Usage Data

Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device advertising ID (GAID)
  • IP address and approximate location (country/region)
  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • In-game events and session statistics
  • Ad interaction data

02 How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your anonymous or Google-linked account
  • Save and sync your game progress across sessions
  • Display leaderboards and rank players
  • Personalise and improve your gaming experience
  • Serve in-game advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Analyse app performance and fix bugs via Firebase Analytics
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

03 Third-Party Services

The Game integrates the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

🔥 Google Firebase

Used for anonymous authentication, Google Sign-In, game data storage (Firestore), and usage analytics (Firebase Analytics).

firebase.google.com/support/privacy →

📢 Google AdMob

Used to display in-game advertisements. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to serve personalised ads. You can opt out on Android via Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

policies.google.com/privacy →

04 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these cases:

  • With Firebase and Google as service providers, strictly to operate the Game
  • With AdMob to deliver in-game advertisements
  • When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public

05 Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Game's services. Anonymous accounts with extended inactivity may be deleted. If you linked your Google account, data is retained until you request deletion.

06 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain data processing
  • Data portability (EU/EEA residents under GDPR)
  • Lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority (EU/EEA residents)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at smygamestudios@gmail.com.

07 Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete it.

08 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and secure Firebase infrastructure. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

09 International Data Transfers

The Game is available worldwide. Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States. By using the Game, you consent to such transfers. For EU/EEA users, transfers are conducted in compliance with GDPR requirements.

Account Deletion

Paws & Clues: Cat Puzzle

You can delete your account and associated game data directly inside the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open Paws & Clues: Cat Puzzle.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.

Data that will be deleted

  • Firebase Authentication account
  • Linked Google sign-in information, if linked
  • Username
  • Leaderboard/profile data
  • Game progress stored in our database

Data retention

Some non-personal technical logs may be retained temporarily for security, fraud prevention, debugging, or legal compliance, and are automatically deleted after the applicable retention period.
